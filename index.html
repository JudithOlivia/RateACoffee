Rate A Coffee

Rating my top 10 favourite cofee types.

1. Iced Cofee

Why I Like Iced Coffee

I'm a huge fan of ice cold drinks and coffee. This is a perfect balance of both.

2. Cappuccino

Why I Like Cappuccino

This has the most perfect texture. Idk how to explain it but it is strong and light/foam-y at the same time.

3. Mocha

Why I Like Mocha

I love chocolate and this drink combines my two favourites: chocolate and coffee.

4. Espresso

Why I Like Espresso

I like the strong flavour it has. Also, the tiny cup it is served in is really cute.

5. Black Coffee

Why I Like Black Coffee

This is my biggest hear me out in cofee. I loveeee the strong flavour it has.

6. Turkish Coffee

Why I Like Turkish Coffee

I love the intense and bold flavour it has. Also has a nice aroma

7. Cold Brew

Why I Like Cold Brew

This doesn't have the bitter laste that iced cofee has. Has a smooth and mellow flavour to it.

8. Frapee

Why I Like Frapee

9. Café au Lait

Why I Like Café au Lait

Its not strong but is light and mild. I like it cuz its comforting.
